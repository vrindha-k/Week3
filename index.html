1968

Intel Founded

Intel is founded by Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel releases the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor.

1978

8086 Processor

Launch of the 8086 processor establishing the x86 architecture.

1985

386 Processor

Intel introduces the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture.

2006

Peak GHG Emissions

Intel begins major investments in emissions reduction.

2020

RISE Strategy

Intel launches its RISE sustainability strategy and 2030 goals.

2022

Net-Zero By 2040

Intel commits to achieving net-zero emissions globally.

2023

Renewable Electricity

Intel reaches 99% renewable electricity usage worldwide.

2024

Sustainability Summit

Intel hosts its first Sustainability Summit.
